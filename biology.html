Declassified Extract 04-A — Biochemical Pathways Under Artificial Spectral Stress

This section draws upon verified NIH and CDC plant toxicology research to illustrate how real biochemical pathways can be destabilized through environmental control systems. The events depicted in Dark Recipe extrapolate from these mechanisms.

Clinical Correlates — Human Physiology

  • Psoralen exposure → delayed erythema under UV-A (320–400 nm).
  • Systemic ingestion → phototoxic dermatitis, ocular sensitivity.
  • Inhaled terpenes (geraniol/limonene) → mucosal irritation, mild neuro-stimulation.
  • Chronic oxidative load → hepatic stress via cytochrome P450 overactivity.
